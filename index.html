Bot Olympics Tasks

#1: Ramp Task

We're able to successfully make the robot climb a ramp, spin one and a half times at the top, come back down, and spin back to its original position.

#2: Figure 8 Task

We're able to make the robot travel in a figure 8 pattern, first going down and clockwise and then going up and counterclockwise.

#3: Drawing 'AV' Task

We're able to make the robot draw the letters 'A' and 'V', even drawing a little line at the bottom to indicate whitespace.

#4: Foot-long Circle Task

We're able to make the robot move in a circle such that the radius of the circle is 12 inches (or 24 inches in diameter, as shown by the ruler).
